Services

What we do

Hand Wash

Snow foam pre-wash, two-bucket hand wash, iron fallout removal, tar decontamination. The proper foundation for any detail.

Paint Correction

Machine polishing to remove swirl marks, scratches and oxidation. Restore the original gloss and depth of your paintwork.

Ceramic Coating

Long-lasting protection up to 5 years. Hydrophobic finish, UV protection, resistance to road salt and minor scratches.

Interior Valeting

Deep clean of fabric, leather, headliner. Stain and odour removal, antibacterial treatment, leather conditioning.

Headlight Restoration

Restore clarity to faded or yellowed headlights. Brighter beam, sharper looks, finished with a protective UV sealant.

Wheels & Tyres

Deep wheel cleaning, brake dust removal, tyre dressing. Optional wheel ceramic coating for easier future cleaning.

About us

King Detailing — royal care for your car in South Hams

We're a team of certified detailers based in Kingsbridge, Devon, with over 5 years of experience. We use only professional-grade products from Koch Chemie, Gyeon, CarPro and Sonax. Every car is individually assessed before any work begins.

Our unit is fully heated, professionally lit (6500K) and equipped with proper extraction. We work clean, careful, and on schedule.
